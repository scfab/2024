Camera-ready Guidelines

These instructions are for the authors of accepted papers. We need the following by April 1st (11:59 PM (PDT)):
  • Your paper MUST be formatted using one of the templates available in the "Call for Papers" section. These template files are a variant of the ACM style that provide a consistent look and feel for our proceedings. Please do not use your own style file or one of the "standard" ACM ones.
  • Your papers should be submitted as .pdfs and the PDF MUST have the correct copyright statement on the bottom of the first page left column which should read as follows:
    This article is published under a Creative Commons Attribution License (http://creativecommons.org/licenses/by/3.0/), which permits distribution and reproduction in any medium as well allowing derivative works, provided that you attribute the original work to the author(s) and FAB 2022.   • Do not include page numbers.
  • Name your file according to this rule: FAB2022-paperID-firstauthorlastname.pdf.
  • The page limit restrictions still stand and are strict and final.
  • Please use the paper-submission site to upload your final camera-ready copy. If you have any other question regarding the proceedings, please contact the publication and proceedings chair or the PC chairs immediately. Don't wait!
  • At least one of the authors must register for the symposium to present their work.
